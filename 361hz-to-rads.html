Formula for 361 Hz to Rad/s

Given a frequency of 361 Hz, the conversion to radians per second (rad/s) can be expressed as:

ω = 2π * f

Where:

  • ω is the angular frequency in rad/s,
  • π is a mathematical constant approximately equal to 3.14159,
  • f is the frequency in Hz.

Substituting the given frequency into the formula:

ω = 2π * 361 Hz

Therefore,

ω = 2268.23 rad/s or 129960.0°/s
