Formula for 3657 Hz to Rad/s

Given a frequency of 3657 Hz, the conversion to radians per second (rad/s) can be expressed as:

ω = 2π * f

Where:

  • ω is the angular frequency in rad/s,
  • π is a mathematical constant approximately equal to 3.14159,
  • f is the frequency in Hz.

Substituting the given frequency into the formula:

ω = 2π * 3657 Hz

Therefore,

ω = 22977.61 rad/s or 1316520.0°/s
