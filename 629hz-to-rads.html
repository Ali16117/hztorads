To convert 629 Hertz to radians per second, Multiply 629Hz by 2π rad. 629 Hz times 2π rad gives you 3952.12 rad/s. Therefore, a frequency of 629 Hz is equivalent to an angular velocity of 3952.12 rad/s.

Formula for 629 Hz to Rad/s

Given a frequency of 629 Hz, the conversion to radians per second (rad/s) can be expressed as:

ω = 2π * f

Where:

  • ω is the angular frequency in rad/s,
  • π is a mathematical constant approximately equal to 3.14159,
  • f is the frequency in Hz.

Substituting the given frequency into the formula:

ω = 2π * 629 Hz

Therefore,

ω = 3952.12 rad/s or 226440.0°/s
