To convert 490 Hertz to radians per second, Multiply 490Hz by 2π rad. 490 Hz times 2π rad gives you 3078.76 rad/s. Therefore, a frequency of 490 Hz is equivalent to an angular velocity of 3078.76 rad/s.

Formula for 490 Hz to Rad/s

Given a frequency of 490 Hz, the conversion to radians per second (rad/s) can be expressed as:

ω = 2π * f

Where:

  • ω is the angular frequency in rad/s,
  • π is a mathematical constant approximately equal to 3.14159,
  • f is the frequency in Hz.

Substituting the given frequency into the formula:

ω = 2π * 490 Hz

Therefore,

ω = 3078.76 rad/s or 176400.0°/s
